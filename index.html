Baale LandScape and Natural Resources

It’s not only endemic wildlife that makes Bale so special – the area embraces sixteen of Ethiopia’s endemic birds including forest-dwelling endemics – the Yellow-fronted Parrot, Abyssinian catbird, Banded barbet and Abyssinian woodpecker. In the Sanatte moorlands, the keen ornithologists are able to spot Blue-winged Goose, Rouget’s rail and Spotbreasted lapwing.
